Welcome to Golden Hex, your one-stop shop for a delightful adventure through the culinary landscapes of Europe and the Mediterranean!
Nestled in the heart of Cary, North Carolina, we bring you the vibrant flavors and traditions of over 60 countries directly to your table.

A World of Flavors Under One Roof.
Step into Golden Hex and embark on a sensory journey. Our shelves are brimming with an extensive selection of imported specialty groceries. We source high-quality products from countries like Poland, Ukraine, Romania, Armenia, Russia, Belarus, Bulgaria, and other regions like the Balkans and the Baltics, extending to France, Italy, and beyond, ensuring authenticity and a taste of faraway lands.

Indulge in European Delights
Grocery Aisle: Dive into a treasure trove of culinary essentials. Find a delightful selection of honey, an array of pickled vegetables and mushrooms, a bounty of fresh fruits, and convenient instant food options. Explore our extensive range of sauces, oils (both refined and unrefined), and mayonnaise to add depth and flavor to your dishes. Stock up on sunflower seeds, a variety of jams and preserves, grains, flours, and noodles for your pantry. Elevate your cooking with our selection of spices and seasonings, perfect for creating international dishes at home. Don't forget to explore our dedicated baby food section!
Sweet Treats: Satisfy your sweet tooth with our delectable assortment of candies and chocolates. Indulge in a mix of sweet delights, pre-packaged candies, creamy caramels, and vibrant marmalades. Explore the unique textures of halva, the airy sweetness of marshmallows (zefir) and pastilles, or lose yourself in the world of decadent chocolate bars and beautifully packaged candy selections.
Seafood Symphony:Explore the bounty of the seas with our impressive selection of fish and seafood. We offer luxurious red and black caviar for a touch of indulgence, alongside a variety of canned fish options, perfect for quick and easy meals. Discover the smoky flavors of dried fish or tantalize your taste buds with our selection of smoked and salted fish. We also offer a wide variety of herring (seledka), sardines, sprats, and delicious fish and roe spreads.
A World of Cheeses and Cold Cuts: Embark on a cheese lover's paradise with our extensive selection of European and Mediterranean cheeses. From creamy and mild to sharp and aged, we have something to delight every palate. Pair your cheese selection with our gourmet cold cuts, featuring a delectable range of salami, smoked meats, and traditional salo. Explore our selection of pâtés, canned meats, and a variety of sausages, including hams, bologna, and sosiski. Sampling is complimentary. 


Freshly Made and Ready to Enjoy

Beyond our extensive grocery selection, Golden Hex offers a variety of prepared foods, perfect for a quick and delicious meal. Hot & Cold Bar: Looking for a hot lunch or a satisfying dinner? Our hot and cold bar features a rotating selection of European and Mediterranean favorites. Savor the comforting warmth of a hearty borscht or indulge in the delicious flavors of cabbage rolls. Sample our flavorful kharcho stew or our juicy cutlets. We also offer creamy options like mushroom cream and traditional rassolnik soup.
Dine-In or Take Away: Enjoy your meal in our comfortable seating area or take it home for a convenient and satisfying dining experience.
Pre-Order Homemade Specialties: Planning a special occasion? We offer a selection of homemade food and sweets, perfect for catering or celebrations. Simply pre-order your desired dishes to ensure you have delicious options on hand for your guests. 




Festive Feasts and Seasonal Delights

Throughout the year, Golden Hex embraces the spirit of various holidays and festivals. We offer a curated selection of special products and unique gift options, allowing you to share the joy of international flavors with loved ones. We are More Than Just Food: Come explore what else we offer! 

Golden Hex extends beyond just groceries. We offer a selection of European and Mediterranean essentials, making it a one-stop shop for all your needs. Discover unique gifts and souvenirs to transport yourself and your loved ones to faraway lands. Explore our selection of herbal remedies and browse our magazine section for international publications.

Unveiling Culinary Treasures

At Golden Hex, we believe food is a language that transcends borders. We're passionate about sharing the rich culinary tapestry of Europe and the Mediterranean with our community. Whether you're a seasoned explorer of international flavors or just embarking on your taste bud adventure, we invite you to discover a world of culinary possibilities at Golden Hex. Visit us today and embark on a delicious journey!
