Exciting News! We MOVED & EXPANDED!

From California Sunshine to Triangle Flavors:



Golden Hex expands with European & Mediterranean delights, becoming the LARGEST European small-business family-owned market in NC! Calling all foodies and culture enthusiasts in the Triangle! Get ready to embark on a delicious journey across Europe and the Mediterranean, right here in Cary, NC. The Golden Hex story began with a California family's passion for honey, evolving into a thriving business offering raw honey, pollen, propolis, beeswax candies, natural cosmetics, and soaps. Upon relocating to North Carolina, we expanded our love for European and Mediterranean flavors. Our first Cary store quickly became a haven for authentic groceries and specialty items, alongside our beloved North Carolina honey.
