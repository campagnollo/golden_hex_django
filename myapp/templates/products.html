PRODUCTS

Golden Hex Foods is a gourmet food store in the Triangle (RTP) region, North Carolina. We specialize in European and Mediterranean foods. Our store offers a wide selection of imported goods, quality meats and seafood, cheeses, dairy products, sweets, and other groceries.

Sweets

Golden Hex Foods offers a variety of sweets and desserts from Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Armenia, Israel, and many other countries. Candies, cookies, cakes, honey, preserves & other delicious desserts available.

Meat, Cheese & Fish

Buy as little or as much as you want. Our owners will help you choose the best cold meats and cheeses, sliced to order or vacuum-sealed. We also sell smoked, marinated, and dry fish, plus red and black caviar!

Marinated Food & Condiments

Marinated and pickled vegetables, canned fish and meats, oils, sauces, and dips imported from around the world.

Drinks, Juices & Syrups

Enjoy flavors from your homeland! Tarhun (tarragon drink), lemonade, kvass, mors, Borjomi, and more.

Tea, Coffee & Chicory

Explore teas and coffee from around the world. Health enthusiasts will find a variety of herbal teas & chicory coffee.

Kosher Food

We proudly bring a new selection of Kosher food to the region, including favorites imported from Israel.
